About US

EXON Systems is a software development company founded by Protekti and MasixRB, two experienced
developers in the Roblox community. The company is focused on providing innovative software
solutions that help improve the development experience for Roblox users.

Their flagship product, EXON Shield, is an anti-virus plugin for Roblox Studio that provides powerful
protection against malicious code and scripts. With features such as quick virus removal, workspace scanning,
and a sleek user interface, EXON Shield is a must-have tool for any Roblox developer looking to safeguard their projects.

EXON Systems' dedication to quality and innovation has made them a trusted name in the Roblox community,
and they continue to push the boundaries of what's possible with their cutting-edge software solutions.
